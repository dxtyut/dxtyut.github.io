# ✨ About Me

I am currently an Associate Professor and Master Supervisor at the [College of Artificial Intelligence](https://tylgswyxgc.tyut.edu.cn/) of [Taiyuan University of Technology](https://www.tyut.edu.cn/), Shanxi, China. I graduated from [School of Big Data & Software Engineering](https://www.cse.cqu.edu.cn/) of [Chongqing University](https://www.cqu.edu.cn/) with a PhD degree, advised by Professors Dan Yang, Xiaohong Zhang and Sheng Huang. 

My research interest includes **Vision Language Model** (VLM), **Computer Vision** (CV), **Face anti-Spoofing** (FAS) and **Image Segmentation**.
