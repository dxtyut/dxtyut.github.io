# ✨ About Me

I am currently an Associate Professor and Master Supervisor at the [College of Artificial Intelligence](https://tylgswyxgc.tyut.edu.cn/) of [Taiyuan University of Technology](https://www.tyut.edu.cn/), Shanxi, China. I graduated from [School of Big Data & Software Engineering](https://www.cse.cqu.edu.cn/), [Chongqing University](https://www.cqu.edu.cn/) with a PhD degree, advised by Dan Yang. 

My research interest includes Visual Language Model (VLM), Computer Vision (CV), Face anti-Spoofing (FAS) and Image Segmentation.
